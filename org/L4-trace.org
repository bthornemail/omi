* Modal Tautology
The key words "MUST", "MUST NOT", "REQUIRED", "SHALL", "SHALL
NOT", "SHOULD", "SHOULD NOT", "RECOMMENDED", "MAY", and
"OPTIONAL" in this layer specification are to be interpreted

** S-P-O Definition (with RFC 2119 Modalities)

| Subject     | Predicate | Object             | MODALITY |
|-------------+-----------+--------------------+----------|
| byte_stream | produces  | Γ (operator sequence) | MUST     |
| Γ           | preserves | execution_order     | MUST     |

** Contract (Normative)
- Input: byte stream — MUST be valid sequence
- Output: Γ (ordered operator sequence) — MUST be mode-independent
- Responsibility: reliable operator sequence delivery — MUST NOT reorder operations
- Invariant: Γ is IDENTICAL across all OMICRON modes — MUST hold for all executions

** OMI Mapping
- Trace is a free monoid over Γ — MUST satisfy monoid properties
- Mode-independent: Γ_linear = Γ_recursive = Γ_replay — MUST be equal
- Γ preserves execution order — MUST NOT change sequence semantics

** Implementation Notes
- The trace Γ is the sequence of gauge operators applied in order — MUST maintain sequence
- This layer ensures that the operator sequence is delivered reliably — MUST not drop or duplicate
- The invariant means that the same input byte stream produces the same Γ — MUST hold regardless of the OMICRON mode (linear, recursive, replay)
- The trace is used by higher layers for projection and interpretation — MUST remain unchanged by upper layers
- Phase 20 Header Algebra observes the ESC-resolved trace only: [[file:PHASE-20-HEADER-ALGEBRA.org][Phase 20 Header]]
