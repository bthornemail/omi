# Phase 79 - Narrative Timeline as Declared World

Phase 79 turns the narrative series into a deterministic declared timeline and
movie surface. This is not video playback. It is a projection walk over
declared relations.

## Status

`.omilisp` remains the canonical narrative authority. Markdown remains human
projection. The narrative timeline derives from the canonical declaration and
the narrative chapter corpus, then lower into deterministic readouts.

## View Law

- `lazy` -> sealed narrative timeline / barcode-like manifest
- `greedy` -> movie surface with scenes, frames, and beats
- `static` -> reconciliation summary over the declared narrative space
- `animated` -> deterministic frame sweep over declared relations

## Invariants

- Timeline frames are declared world states, not rendered bitmaps.
- Chapters emit deterministic scenes and beats.
- Interjections from the aside surface are overlay candidates only.
- S-P-O-M triplets are derived from narrative relations with modality.
- Identity receipts anchor the declared timeline.
- Projection receipts change with the readout lens.
- View receipts change with the presentation surface.

## Narrative Surface

The narrative declaration becomes a declared world graph with:

- chapter order
- scene order
- beat order
- interjection overlays
- triplet-derived relations

That gives the workbench a deterministic movie surface without collapsing the
canonical narrative into a renderer or player.
